About Us

PrevNext

Hotel Africana is Uganda ‘s Premier luxury Hotel. 40 minutes drive from Entebbe International Airport and 3 minutes drive from the city center. Our location makes you feel at home from the moment you walk in featuring a panoramic view of the city skyline and surrounding hilltops.
Since July 1997, the hotel has become a symbol of excellent sophistication and high quality services in:-

  • Conference facilities.
  • Luxurious rooms.
  • Wedding reception facilities.
  • Health club and golfing amenities.
  • Restaurant and lounges.
  • Shopping malls.

Hotel Africana offers a wide choice of tasty dishes ranging from: oriental, western, culinary & Africana delicacies. The hotel mission statement is to provide quality and professional management service for superior hospitality
